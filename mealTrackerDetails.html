Meal Tracker

A common goal a lot of people have is being fit and healthy. Arguably, the best way to get to that goal is with what you are eating. Or better yet, what the food you eat is fundamently composed of. My Meal Tracker App, lets you input not only what you are eating, but it also gives the option to see what your daily calories look like being broken up by your breakfast/lunch/dinner.
