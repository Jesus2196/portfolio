Into Infinity

Being a huge fan of space exploration myself, it was very likely sooner or later I was going to do a space themed app. Together with two other space fans, we sought out and created a space themed educational application where you can input data for others to see and learn from your personal knowledge but it is also divided up by space objects and missions, that can be assigned to or removed from other objects.
